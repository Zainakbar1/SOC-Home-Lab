# Executive Summary

An SMB enumeration activity originating from the Kali attack workstation was detected against the Windows endpoint. The objective of the exercise was to simulate reconnaissance commonly performed before lateral movement.

The activity included host discovery, port scanning, SMB version detection, and share enumeration attempts. Telemetry generated by these actions was collected through Sysmon and analyzed in Splunk Enterprise.
